Before renting, inspect the car thoroughly for any pre-existing damage and ensure all features are functioning correctly. Report any issues to our staff immediately to avoid potential charges.

Documents Required for car rental

To rent a car, ensure you have a valid driver's license and a form of identification such as a passport or ID card. Additionally, be prepared to provide a credit card for payment and proof of insurance if required by the rental company.
